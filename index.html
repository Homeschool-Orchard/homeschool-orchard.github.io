Welcome to Homeschool Orchard! After working with hundreds of homeschool families, teachers, education specialists, and college admissions officers, we are excited to be launching our platform soon: your one-stop solution for all your homeschooling needs. We offer a wide range of tools, including curriculum planning and scheduling, record keeping, college application materials, and state specific compliance materials generation.

Our Homeschool Charter and ISP Services

At Homeschool Orchard, we make it easier for ISPs' and charter schools' Education Specialists/Teachers to work with their homeschool families through a seamless platform and material aggregation and automation system, allowing families and teachers to spend less time on busywork and more time learning and growing

Curriculum Planner & Scheduler

We make it easier for homeschool families to plan their coursework, and provide checks for state compliance and specific college requirements

Record Keeping

We make it easier for homeschool families to upload their course materials and for Education Specialists to evaluate work product

Document Generation

Through direct work with ISPs and homeschool charter schools, we can now automatically generate documents for state compliance
